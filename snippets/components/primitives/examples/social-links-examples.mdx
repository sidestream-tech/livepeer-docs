## SocialLinks

Displays a row of social media icon links for Livepeer platforms.

### Basic Usage

<SocialLinks />

### Custom Size

<SocialLinks size={24} />

<SocialLinks size={32} />

### Left Aligned

<SocialLinks justify="flex-start" />

### Right Aligned

<SocialLinks justify="flex-end" />

### Custom Gap

<SocialLinks gap="1.5rem" />

### With Custom Color

<SocialLinks color="var(--accent)" />

### In a Card

<Card title="Connect With Us" icon="users">
  Join the Livepeer community on your favorite platform:
  
  <SocialLinks size={24} gap="1rem" />
</Card>

### In Footer Style

<div style={{ 
  backgroundColor: "var(--card-background)", 
  padding: "1.5rem", 
  borderRadius: "8px",
  textAlign: "center"
}}>
  <p style={{ marginBottom: "0.5rem", fontWeight: "bold" }}>Follow Livepeer</p>

</div>
