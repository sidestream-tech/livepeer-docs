## Styling Notes

These callouts use **semantic colors** that are intentionally fixed:

| Callout | Color | Purpose |
|---------|-------|---------|
| ComingSoonCallout | Pink (`#ef1a73`) | Work in progress |
| PreviewCallout | Purple (`#b636dd`) | Under construction |
| ReviewCallout | Purple (`#b636dd`) | Needs review |

The colors remain consistent across light/dark themes to maintain their semantic meaning.
