## CardCarousel

A simple carousel that paginates through cards with navigation controls.

### Basic Usage (3 visible)

<CardCarousel visibleCount={3}>
  <Card title="Card 1" icon="video">First card content</Card>
  <Card title="Card 2" icon="code">Second card content</Card>
  <Card title="Card 3" icon="server">Third card content</Card>
  <Card title="Card 4" icon="rocket">Fourth card content</Card>
  <Card title="Card 5" icon="star">Fifth card content</Card>
  <Card title="Card 6" icon="book">Sixth card content</Card>
</CardCarousel>
