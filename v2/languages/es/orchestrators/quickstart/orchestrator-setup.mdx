<PreviewCallout />

Este inicio rápido pone tu GPU en la red Livepeer ejecutando el **go-livepeer** cliente en modo orquestador. Instalarás (o usarás Docker), te conectarás a Arbitrum, establecerás precios y activarás. Para una guía completa de instalación (binario, Docker, fuente), consulta [Instalar go-livepeer](/v2/orchestrators/setup/install-go-livepeer). Para conectarte a Arbitrum (RPC, alojado vs autoalojado), consulta [Conectar a Arbitrum](/v2/orchestrators/setup/install-go-livepeer).

## Requisitos previos

- Familiaridad con la línea de comandos; `wget` o `curl`; [grpcurl](https://github.com/fullstorydev/grpcurl) opcional para pruebas.
- [Controlador NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx) instalado si se utiliza transcodificación con GPU.
- Acceso a un [Arbitrum punto final RPC](../setting-up-an-orchestrator/connect-to-arbitrum) (alojado o autoalojado).
- **go-livepeer** [instalado](/v2/orchestrators/setup/install-go-livepeer) (binario o Docker).

<Note> Non-NVIDIA GPU support may be added in the future. This guide assumes NVIDIA GPUs for transcoding.</Note>

<StyledSteps>
  <StyledStep title="List your GPUs">
    Before starting the node, confirm your GPUs are visible. With the NVIDIA driver installed, run:

    <CustomCodeBlock
      codeString={`nvidia-smi -L`}
      language="bash"
      icon="terminal"
    />

    Example output (5 GPUs, IDs 0–4):

    <CustomCodeBlock
      codeString={`GPU 0: GeForce GTX 1070 Ti (UUID: GPU-fcbaffa0-...)
GPU 1: GeForce GTX 1070 Ti (UUID: GPU-d46a085e-...)
...`}
      language="text"
      icon="terminal"
    />
  </StyledStep>

  <StyledStep title="Run livepeer in orchestrator mode">
    Start the node with the required flags. Replace placeholders with your values.

    <CustomCodeBlock
      codeString={`livepeer \\
  -network arbitrum-one-mainnet \\
  -ethUrl <ETH_URL> \\
  -orchestrator \\
  -transcoder \\
  -nvidia <NVIDIA_DEVICE_IDS> \\
  -pricePerUnit <PRICE_PER_UNIT> \\
  -serviceAddr <HOSTNAME_OR_IP:8935>`}
      language="bash"
      icon="terminal"
    />

    <Accordion title="Key flags" icon="list">
      - **-ethUrl** - Arbitrum RPC URL. See [Connect to Arbitrum](../setting-up-an-orchestrator/connect-to-arbitrum).
      - **-ethAcctAddr** - (Optional) ETH account address if you already have a keystore.
      - **-orchestrator** / **-transcoder** - Run as orchestrator with GPU transcoding.
      - **-nvidia** - Comma-separated GPU IDs (e.g. `0,1,2`). Omit for CPU-only.
      - **-pricePerUnit** - Price in wei per pixel. Required at startup; can be changed later.
      - **-serviceAddr** - Public address for incoming requests (hostname:port preferred over IP).
    </Accordion>

    <Tip> Keystore default: `~/.lpData/arbitrum-one-mainnet/keystore`. If no account exists, you will be prompted to create one and set a passphrase. Guard the private key and passphrase; they control your wallet and funds.</Tip>
  </StyledStep>

  <StyledStep title="Confirm the node is listening">
    When the node is ready, logs should show:

    <CustomCodeBlock
      codeString={`Listening for RPC on :8935
Received Ping request`}
      language="text"
      icon="terminal"
    />
  </StyledStep>

  <StyledStep title="Fund your account">
    To activate on Livepeer you need:
    - **ETH** (Arbitrum) - for transaction fees. Start with a small amount; the CLI will warn if balance is low.
    - **LPT** - to stake and join the active set. Check the [Explorer](https://explorer.livepeer.org/) for the 100th orchestrator’s stake; you need at least that much to enter the top 100.

    <Warning> If your orchestrator leaves the active set (top 100 by stake), it will not be reactivated automatically unless you re-register or your stake changes so you re-enter the top 100.</Warning>
  </StyledStep>

  <StyledStep title="Activate your orchestrator">
    Use **livepeer_cli** or the [Explorer](https://explorer.livepeer.org/) to bond LPT to yourself, set reward cut, fee share, price, and service address. Once transactions confirm, your orchestrator joins the active set in the next round.

    See [Staking LPT](../advanced-setup/staking-LPT) and [Rewards and fees](../advanced-setup/rewards-and-fees) for economics. Full activation steps are in the [Install guide](/v2/orchestrators/setup/install-go-livepeer#activate).
  </StyledStep>
</StyledSteps>
