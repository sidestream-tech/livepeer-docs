## Roadmap

As AI models evolve toward [world models](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/glossary/world-models/), autonomous agents, and real-time simulation, demand for open GPU infrastructure continues to be exponential.

Livepeer meet this dynamic need and is expanding its interactive media capabilities - embedding provenance, trust & authenticity into world models and the Livepeer network.

Livepeer is building the **open execution layer** for internet-scale *real-time media experiences*.

Affordable. Performant. Dynamically Scalable.
