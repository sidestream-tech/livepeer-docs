Diagram: Orchestrator Architecture – the gateway assigns jobs to an orchestrator, which splits work to either a video transcoder process or an AI inference process running on its GPUs, then returns the result. Verification (for transcoding) and other coordination happen inside the orchestrator’s controller.
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    A[**Source (App or Stream)**] -->|ingest video + job request| G((**Livepeer Gateway**))
    G -->|find suitable node| O{{**Orchestrator Node**}}
    subgraph Orchestrator Node
        direction TB
        OR[Orchestrator<br/>Controller] -->|video segment| TR[Transcoder<br/>GPU Worker]
        OR[Orchestrator<br/>Controller] -->|AI task| AIW[AI Inference<br/>GPU Worker]
        TR -->|encoded outputs| OR
        AIW -->|inference outputs| OR
        OR -->|verification & packaging| OR
    end
    O -->|transcoded video or AI result| G
    G -->|deliver stream| V[**Viewer / Application**]
```
