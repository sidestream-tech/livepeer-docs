Livepeer supports several operator paths, and the right starting point depends on whether you want to contribute GPU capacity, run your own node, or manage a larger fleet. Use this page to choose the path that matches your hardware, LPT position, and operating model before you start setup.

{/* REVIEW: Task brief references archived or non-live routes such as /v2/orchestrators/guides/join-a-pool and /v2/orchestrators/get-started/quickstart-views/orchestrator-setup. This page uses the current live IA equivalents instead. */}

## Choose your path

| Path | LPT stake required | Hardware | Difficulty | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pool Worker | None | Any NVIDIA GPU accepted by a pool | Low | Getting started without staking or running protocol infrastructure |
| Solo Orchestrator | Yes | NVIDIA GPU with NVENC; best fit for 1-4 consumer GPUs | Medium | Direct control over your own node and earnings |
| Pool Operator | Yes | 4-20+ GPUs or an external worker fleet | High | Operators accepting third-party GPUs and managing shared payouts |
| Enterprise / Data Centre | Yes | Hosted, colocated, or multi-region GPU fleet | Very high | Cloud, data-centre, and fleet operators |
