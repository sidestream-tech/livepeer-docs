Running an orchestrator means operating a **go-livepeer** node that provides GPU compute (video transcoding and/or AI inference) to the Livepeer network. You earn from protocol rewards (LPT) and job fees (ETH).

## Setup checklist

1. **Hardware** - [Hardware requirements](/v2/orchestrators/setup/rcs-requirements)
2. **Install** - [Install go-livepeer](/v2/orchestrators/setup/rs-install)
3. **Connect** - [Connect to Arbitrum](/v2/orchestrators/setup/sc-connect)
4. **Configure** - [Configure your node](/v2/orchestrators/setup/r-configure)
5. **Run and activate** - [Activate on the network](/v2/orchestrators/unclassified/rcs-connect-activate-publish)
6. **Monitor** - [Orchestrator stats and monitoring](/v2/orchestrators/setup/r-monitor)
