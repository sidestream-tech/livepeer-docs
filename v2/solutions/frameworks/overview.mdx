Frameworks is **full-stack video infrastructure** from a Livepeer Special Purpose Entity (SPE). The only streaming platform that combines full self-hosting with hosted processing-run it yourself, use Frameworks’ hosted services, or mix and match. Uncloud your infrastructure. Powered by MistServer and Livepeer.
