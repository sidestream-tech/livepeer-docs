Frameworks is **full-stack video infrastructure** from a Livepeer Special Purpose Entity (SPE). It combines open-source, self-hosted streaming with optional hosted processing-so you can run everything yourself, use Frameworks’ hosted services, or mix and match. It is powered by MistServer and the Livepeer network.

## What you can do

- **Sovereign streaming** - Self-host the entire stack with no vendor lock-in. SaaS → hybrid (self-hosted edge) → fully self-hosted; one platform, three modes.
- **Developer-first platform** - GraphQL API, typed SDKs, universal playback, and StreamCrafter (OBS-style compositing in the browser). WebRTC-first for sub-second latency.
- **Agent-native** - Discover via SKILL.md and MCP, authenticate with wallet or x402, pay with USDC/card/crypto. Operate streams, diagnostics, and billing via MCP.
- **Analytics and ops** - Routing decisions, QoE metrics, player telemetry. Skipper: documentation and stream diagnostics with live stream checks.

## How it fits with Livepeer

Frameworks runs on Livepeer for transcoding and compute. The free tier includes self-hosting plus access to a shared bandwidth pool with Livepeer-backed compute. For GPU-heavy features (AI processing, multi-stream compositing) and hosted services, paid tiers use the same network.
