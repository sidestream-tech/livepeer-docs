This tab serves as a guide to the thought process behind how the docs were defined and structured in relation to the Livepeer Protocol, Platforms, Network, Ecosystem and the actors within them.

It additionally defines provided strategic alignment goals with Livepeer objectives, an RFP review and finalisation report and future work recommendations.

Additionally this section acts as a human readable portal for reports produced by script outputs in this repo, including WCAG, broken links, usefulness, translation & more.

Specifically

- philopsophy & aims of documentation
- understanding of livepeer strategic objectives & alignment in docs
- persona definitions
- persona journeys
- livepeer ecosystem interactions & needs
