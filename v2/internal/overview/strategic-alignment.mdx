## Overview

Main Objective of any startup is to onboard paying users.
Related core objective - provide a product people want & love

At Livepeer - the core product/s is/are real-time video and, more recently, AI pipelines.
As a decentralised network, Livepeer must compete with both other decentralised services and with centralised counterparts.
It needs to ensure the key value propositions are clear for users coming from diverse backgrounds and - importantly - for potential partners interested in integrating Livepeer as a solution.
