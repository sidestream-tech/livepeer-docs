## Explorer

For your dual AI/video onchain gateway, you can view orchestrator services in two main locations:

1. **Orchestrators Page** (`/orchestrators/orchestrators-portal`) - Shows all orchestrators with financial metrics and ROI projections [1](#0-0)
2. **Performance Leaderboard** (`/leaderboard`) - Displays orchestrators ranked by operational performance with AI/transcoding pipeline options [2](#0-1)

---

## Most Relevant Information for Your Gateway

### Performance Metrics
The Performance Leaderboard is particularly valuable for your dual gateway as it allows you to filter by:
- **Region** - Find orchestrators closest to your users for optimal latency [3](#0-2)
- **Pipeline** - Switch between transcoding and AI pipelines to see specialized performance [4](#0-3)
- **Model** - For AI workloads, filter by specific models [5](#0-4)

Key performance indicators displayed:
- **Total Score (0-10)** - Composite performance metric [6](#0-5)
- **Success Rate (%)** - Job completion reliability [7](#0-6)
- **Latency Score (0-10)** - Round-trip time performance [8](#0-7)

### Economic Information
For cost optimization and understanding orchestrator economics:
- **Price per Pixel** - Current advertised transcoding prices in WEI [9](#0-8)
- **Fee Cut** - Percentage of fees kept by orchestrator vs distributed to delegators [10](#0-9)
- **Reward Cut** - Percentage of inflationary rewards retained [11](#0-10)

### Detailed Orchestrator Views
Click any orchestrator to see detailed statistics including:
- Top regional scores for both transcoding and AI pipelines [12](#0-11)
- Total delegated stake indicating network trust [13](#0-12)
- Reward call reliability (30-round history) [14](#0-13)

## Notes

The Performance Leaderboard is especially useful for your use case as it provides real-time operational metrics that directly impact gateway performance, while the main Orchestrators page helps with understanding the economic landscape and potential delegation opportunities.
