## Livepeer Studio Clients 

Livepeer’s commercial offering, Livepeer Studio, has attracted multiple Web2 and Web3 platforms,spanning from social media to live entertainment, each bringing their user base’s video traffic onto the decentralized network.


- SankoTV – a live social streaming platform – moved off AWS’s IVS to Livepeer, achieving ~80% cost savings

- Fishtank LIVE – a 24/7 live reality show – migrated from Cloudflare to Livepeer Studio, cutting streaming costs by ~50% and improving quality. Founder Bill Ottman praised “improved quality, cost, support and developer tools… [we] slashed video streaming costs by half”

- Switchboard Live – a live stream management (multistreaming) platform – adopted Livepeer Studio as a more cost-effective backend. CEO Rudy Ellis said Livepeer was “more cost-effective, reliable and scalable… with rapid launch time and simple migration”, enabling them to better serve customers across sports, media, education, etc.

- Minds – a Web3 social network – and The Lot Radio – an online radio station with video – are also cited as using Livepeer Studio’s infrastructure

- MyPrize – an interactive event platform – similarly leverages Livepeer for live video
