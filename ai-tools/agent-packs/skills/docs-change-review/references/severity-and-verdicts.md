# Severity And Verdicts

## Severity

- Critical: materially false or dangerous guidance, or a change that breaks the core reader task
- High: strong risk of reader misdirection, duplicate canonical ownership, or major workflow confusion
- Medium: meaningful clarity, metadata, or structural issue with limited immediate harm
- Low: polish issue, minor wording drift, or optional consistency improvement

## Verdicts

- `ready`: no blocking factual or structural issues found
- `ready with follow-up`: safe to merge, but unresolved medium or low issues remain
- `not ready`: at least one critical or high issue remains, or validation is too incomplete to trust the page

## Response Order

1. Findings first
2. Open questions or assumptions
3. Short verdict and validation status
