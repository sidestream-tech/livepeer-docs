# Propagation Checklist

- Identify the canonical owner of the claim.
- Check `tasks/research/claims/*.json` for an existing tracked record first.
- Find direct pages that restate the claim.
- Check glossaries and terminology pages for matching wording.
- Check tutorials and examples that rely on the old behavior.
- Check comparison, FAQ, and decision pages for derivative language.
- Check whether the same fact appears with different numbers, dates, or certainty in active pages.
- Check navigation labels or summary cards only if they encode the factual claim itself.
- Prefer links back to the canonical owner over duplicated re-explanations.
- Record anything not fixed immediately in a follow-up queue with exact paths.
