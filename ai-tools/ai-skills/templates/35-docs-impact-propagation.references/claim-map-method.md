# Claim Map Method

Build impact from the claim family outward.

## Start With The Claim

Write the changed statement as:

- entity or term
- exact behavior, limit, or support statement
- source that changed it

Example:

- entity: gateway support programme
- claim: operators can apply for funding through X process
- source: official programme update

## Then Map Dependents

Look for these page classes:

- canonical owner page
- getting started pages
- comparison or decision pages
- architecture and concept pages
- tutorials and examples
- glossary entries
- FAQ and troubleshooting pages
- funding, support, or roadmap pages
- pages that present the same fact with different dates, numbers, or certainty

## Classification

- `update now`: page repeats or depends directly on the changed claim
- `verify only`: page references the area but may not restate the claim
- `follow-up`: page is affected but needs more context or a larger rewrite
- `historical-review`: page may be intentionally historical or deprecated but still depends on the claim family
