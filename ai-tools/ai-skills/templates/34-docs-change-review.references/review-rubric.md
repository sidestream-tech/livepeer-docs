# Review Rubric

Review in this order.

## 1. Factual Integrity

- Are key claims verified, current, and internally consistent?
- Does the page overstate certainty?
- Does it confuse product behavior, roadmap, support, or payment concepts?

## 2. Ownership And Duplication

- Does the page own a clear question?
- Is it duplicating another canonical page?
- Does related content point readers to the correct owner?

## 3. Page-Type Fit

- Tutorial: outcome-first, runnable, minimal theory
- How-to: procedural and task-specific
- Concept: mental model and internals, not setup
- Reference: exact details, minimal narrative
- Overview: orienting value and boundaries

## 4. Claim Presentation Quality

- Does the page present volatile claims as if they are stable?
- Does it use exact dates where recency matters?
- Does it blur historical and current truth?

## 5. Reader Clarity

- Scope is explicit
- Jargon is controlled
- Visuals are introduced and interpreted
- Next action after reading is obvious
